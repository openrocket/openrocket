§ FILE_VERSION_DIVISOR

final int net.sf.openrocket.file.openrocket.OpenRocketSaver.FILE_VERSION_DIVISOR = 100
static

Divisor used in converting an integer version to the point-represented version. The integer version divided by this value is the major version and the remainder is the minor version. For example 101 corresponds to file version "1.1".
