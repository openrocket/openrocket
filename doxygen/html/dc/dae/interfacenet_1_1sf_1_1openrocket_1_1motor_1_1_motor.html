§ MARGINAL_THRUST

final double net.sf.openrocket.motor.Motor.MARGINAL_THRUST = 0.05
static

Below what portion of maximum thrust is the motor chosen to be off when calculating average thrust and burn time. NFPA 1125 defines the "official" burn time to be the time which the motor produces over 5% of its maximum thrust.
