Detailed Description

A background worker that runs the optimization in the background. It supports providing evaluation and step counter information via listeners that are executed on the EDT.

abstract void net.sf.openrocket.gui.dialogs.optimization.OptimizationWorker.done ( OptimizationException  exception)
abstractprotected

This method is called after the optimization has ended, either normally, when interrupted or by throwing an exception. This method is called on the EDT, like the done() method of SwingWorker.

All data chunks to the listeners will be guaranteed to have been processed before calling done().

Parameters
exceptiona possible optimization exception that occurred, or null for normal exit.
