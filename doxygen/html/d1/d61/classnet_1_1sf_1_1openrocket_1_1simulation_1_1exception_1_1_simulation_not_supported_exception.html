Detailed Description

A exception that signifies that the attempted simulation is not supported. The reason for not being supported may be due to unsupported combination of simulator/calculator, unsupported rocket structure or other reasons.

This exception signifies a fatal problem in the simulation; for non-fatal conditions add a warning to the simulation results.
