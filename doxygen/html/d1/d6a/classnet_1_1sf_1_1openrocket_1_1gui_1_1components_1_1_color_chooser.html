Detailed Description

A panel implementation of a color chooser. The panel has a label and a textfield. The label identifies what the color is to be used for (the purpose), and the textfield is uneditable but has its background set to the currently chosen color as a way of visualizing the color.

The chosen color may be retrieved via a call to getCurrentColor.
