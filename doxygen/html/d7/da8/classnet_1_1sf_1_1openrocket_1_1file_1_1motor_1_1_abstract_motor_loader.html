Calculate the mass of a motor at distinct points in time based on the initial total mass, propellant weight and thrust.

This calculation assumes that the velocity of the exhaust remains constant during the burning. This derives from the mass-flow and thrust relation 

F = m' * v
Parameters
timelist of time points
thrustthrust at the discrete times
totaltotal weight of the motor
