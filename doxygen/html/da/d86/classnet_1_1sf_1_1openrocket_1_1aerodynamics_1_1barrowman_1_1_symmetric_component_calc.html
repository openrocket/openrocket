Detailed Description

Calculates the aerodynamic properties of a SymmetricComponent.

CP and CNa are calculated by the Barrowman method extended to account for body lift by the method presented by Galejs. Supersonic CNa and CP are assumed to be the same as the subsonic values.

Calculates the non-axial forces produced by the fins (normal and side forces, pitch, yaw and roll moments, CP position, CNa).

This method uses the Barrowman method for CP and CNa calculation and the extension presented by Galejs for the effect of body lift.

The CP and CNa at supersonic speeds are assumed to be the same as those at subsonic speeds.
